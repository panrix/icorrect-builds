## Mike McAdam Settlement (Resolved Dec 2025)

### Background
Mike McAdam was employed as Technician and Technical Manager since Oct 2021. Settlement negotiations began Nov 2025 after performance issues and a critical incident where Mike attempted to export the entire Monday.com account data (47 boards including private boards, client records, supplier contracts, employee records, financial data).

### Resolution
- Settlement agreement signed via ACAS framework
- Total cost: ~£10,000+ (wages owed, notice pay, settlement payment, legal fees)
- Departure completed Dec 2025
- Positive reference provided as part of agreement

### Key Lessons
1. **Employment contracts must be in place** — the lack of formal contracts made the situation more complex
2. **Data access controls needed** — Monday.com permissions should restrict account-level exports
3. **KPIs and performance management** — formal systems prevent situations escalating to this point

### Meeting Rhythm (Ali's 5A's Framework)

**MMM — Monday Morning Meeting (Weekly)**
- Full team meeting
- Everyone declares 3-6 most important activities for the week
- Team can add/subtract/change priorities

**FAD — Friday Afternoon Debrief (Weekly)**
- Check in on the 3-6 things declared Monday
- Report: done / not done / why / carrying forward

**Quarterly Reset**
- Revisit the 3-6 big quarterly goals
- Assess progress
- Set next quarter's 3-6 big things
