## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

iCorrect receives ~2,800 inbound calls over 6 months (~112/week). **33% go completely unanswered — nobody picks up.** Of the callers who are missed, 55% never get through at all. Ferrari (Ext 205) answers 61% of all picked-up calls, but his answer rate is declining and the lunch hour (12-1pm) is a black hole at 26%. An AI call agent would immediately capture 35 missed calls/week, with conservative revenue recovery of £1,400/week (£72,800/year).

### Revenue Recovery Model

| Metric | Conservative | Moderate | Optimistic |

| **Annual revenue recovered** | **£36,400** | **£68,250** | **£109,200** |

### Agent Feature Requirements

Based on the data patterns:

1. **24/7 availability** — 332 out-of-hours calls in 6 months, currently 65% missed
2. **Instant pickup** — no ring delay, answer within 2 rings
3. **Quote capability** — most calls are pricing enquiries. Agent needs access to repair pricing
4. **Booking capability** — can confirm appointments and create Monday items
5. **Escalation path** — transfer to Ferrari/workshop for complex cases
6. **Callback scheduling** — if the agent can't resolve, book a callback slot
7. **SMS follow-up** — send confirmation/quote via text after the call
8. **Caller ID lookup** — check if existing customer, pull repair history
9. **Monday integration** — no calls for which we have no follow up
