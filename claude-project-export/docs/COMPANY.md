# COMPANY.md — Business Profile

## Revenue Streams

| Stream | Share | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| Back Market | ~60% | Buy devices through Back Market trade-in from consumers. Refurbish and resell on Back Market platform. |
| Direct customers (non-corporate) | ~30% | Walk-in and mail-in repairs from individual customers. |
| Corporate accounts | ~10% | Repairs for business clients with ongoing accounts. |

Back Market is the dominant revenue channel. Agents should prioritise Back Market operations accordingly.

---

## What We Do

- **Original parts repair:** Board-level component repair using original Apple parts. Not aftermarket swaps.
- **MacBook refurbishment:** Buy, refurbish, and resell through Back Market.
- **Back Market trade-in/resell:** Purchase consumer devices via Back Market trade-in programme, grade, repair, resell.
- **Corporate repairs:** Ongoing accounts with business clients for fleet device repair.

---

## What We Don't Do

- **Non-Apple devices.** No Android, no Windows, no anything else.
- **Very old Apple devices.** If it's too old to be worth repairing, we don't touch it.
