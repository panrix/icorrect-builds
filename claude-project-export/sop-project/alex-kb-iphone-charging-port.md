# iPhone Charging Port Repair

## What We Do
We repair iPhone charging ports using original Apple parts. Before replacing, we run a full power test to check whether the fault is the port itself or something deeper in the charging circuit (charging IC, logic board). If the port is confirmed as the issue, it is replaced same day. If the fault runs deeper, we upgrade to a full diagnostic.

## FAQs

### How do I know if my charging port needs repair?
Signs: only charges when cable is angled, slow/intermittent charging, port feels loose, corrosion visible, moisture warning that won't clear. We test with known-good cables and a power ammeter to confirm.

### How does this compare to the Apple Store?
Apple often replaces the full rear housing (up to £500) even when only the port is faulty. We replace just the port.

### Can a bad charging port damage my iPhone?
Yes. A worn or shorted port can send irregular voltage to the charging IC or logic board, causing overheating or power failure. Early repair prevents more expensive damage.

### What if the issue is not the port?
We test immediately with a power ammeter. If the port passes, we upgrade to a full diagnostic covering charging IC, logic board circuits, and battery system. Full report and quote before proceeding.

### Can you repair a port with liquid damage?
Yes, but this is handled as a liquid damage diagnostic. Liquid faults often affect more than the port, so we inspect and clean the full area.

### What does the warranty cover?
Covers the port and workmanship for two years. Does not cover accidental damage, liquid ingress, or faults from non-original chargers.
