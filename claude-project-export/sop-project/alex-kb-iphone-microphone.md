# iPhone Microphone Repair

## What We Do
iPhones have multiple microphones handling different functions (calls, stereo audio, noise cancellation). We test each microphone independently to identify the exact faulty one, then replace it with an original part. Common causes of failure: liquid damage, drops, or poor third-party repairs (especially rear glass or screen replacements using lasers/heat). Most repairs completed in 2-3 hours.

## FAQs

### Why is my microphone not working during calls or recordings?
One of the multiple microphones has likely failed. Common causes: physical damage, liquid ingress, or poor screen/rear glass repairs that disturbed internal components.

### Can you fix microphone issues after a screen or rear glass repair?
Yes. We frequently see mic failures from heat or laser damage during other repairs. We inspect and confirm before fixing.

### What if more than one microphone is not working?
If multiple mics or all sound input has failed, we run a full diagnostic to check for logic board or audio circuit damage.

### Will my iPhone work normally after repair?
Yes. We check every microphone during post-repair testing: recording quality, noise cancellation, call clarity, and FaceTime.

### Will a failed microphone reduce resale value?
Yes. Mic functionality is tested in all trade-in evaluations. A faulty mic can lower the device grade or mark it as BER.
