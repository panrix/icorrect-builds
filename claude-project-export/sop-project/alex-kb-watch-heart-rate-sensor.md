# Apple Watch Heart Rate Sensor Repair

## What We Do
We repair the Apple Watch heart rate sensor by replacing the damaged module with an original part. The sensor sits on the back of the watch and is essential for heart rate monitoring, activity tracking, ECG readings, and accurate health data. Apple typically recommends full device replacement for this fault, but we replace just the affected module, keeping the rest of the device intact. The back sensor is securely bonded and sealed to retain water resistance.

## FAQs

### Can a cracked sensor stop heart rate tracking?
Yes. If the sensor glass is cracked, missing, or the module is damaged, it can affect tracking accuracy or stop it entirely. ECG data may also be incorrect.

### Do I need to replace my whole Apple Watch if the sensor is damaged?
No. While Apple may recommend full device replacement, we replace just the heart rate sensor module using an original part. It is faster, cheaper, and keeps your watch's data and setup intact.

### Will my Apple Watch still be water resistant after repair?
Yes. We reseal the sensor area using Apple-grade adhesive to maintain water resistance for daily use, including washing hands, light swimming, and workouts.

### What if my sensor stopped working after a drop?
Impact damage is a common cause of sensor failure. If the rear glass is cracked or the watch was dropped, we recommend a sensor inspection and repair to prevent further internal issues.
