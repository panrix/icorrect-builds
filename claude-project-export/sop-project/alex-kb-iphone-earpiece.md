# iPhone Earpiece Speaker Repair

## What We Do
We replace the earpiece speaker (top speaker used for phone calls) using genuine Apple parts. Before repair, we test the speaker, proximity sensor, and front mic to confirm the fault. Common failure in iPhone 12 and 15 series. Most repairs completed in 2-3 hours. If the issue is deeper (microphone array or logic board), we upgrade to a diagnostic.

- Price: £79-£119 (model-dependent)
- Turnaround: 2-3 hours (same day)
- Parts: original Apple earpiece modules
- Warranty: 2 years

## FAQs

### What are the signs my earpiece needs repair?
Cannot hear callers unless on loudspeaker or Bluetooth. Muffled sound, crackling, or total silence from the top speaker.

### Can moisture damage the earpiece?
Yes. Even small amounts of liquid entering the top speaker grill can cause failure. Common in iPhone 12 range. We check for corrosion and clean the area during repair.

### Will I lose Face ID during this repair?
No. Face ID and front camera modules are mounted in the same bracket. We remove and reinstall them properly during the process.

### What if the issue is not the speaker?
If testing shows the fault is with the screen or logic board, we upgrade to a full diagnostic with a quote and repair plan. No parts swapped unnecessarily.

### Will my microphone or FaceTime audio be affected?
No. We test all surrounding components before and after repair, including microphone, proximity sensor, and front camera.

### What does the warranty cover?
Two-year warranty covering the part and repair. Includes distortion, failure, or improper fit. Does not cover accidental or liquid damage.
