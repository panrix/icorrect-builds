# MacBook Touch Bar and Touch ID Repair

## Touch Bar

### What We Do
We repair MacBook Touch Bars by replacing the specific component at fault, whether that is the Touch Bar itself or a damaged component on the logic board. Unlike Apple, we do not replace the full top case. We use original Apple parts, and every repair is tested for brightness, response accuracy, and touch input before return.

- Price: from £179 (model-dependent); see pricing/macbook.md
- Turnaround: 2-3 working days standard; 1 working day express (book in advance)
- Parts: original Apple Touch Bar assemblies
- Warranty: 2 years

### FAQs

#### Why is my Touch Bar not lighting up or responding?
The Touch Bar is made up of a display strip and a touch sensor module. If either fails, or if liquid enters the connection point near the Touch ID button, the entire Touch Bar can stop working. It connects directly to the logic board, so ongoing faults can eventually damage the board.

#### Can liquid damage affect the Touch Bar?
Yes. Moisture commonly enters through the gap between the Touch Bar and Touch ID. Even a small amount can affect the touch sensors, display, or the connector to the logic board. If there has been any contact with moisture, we recommend a diagnostic.

#### Do you replace the whole top case like Apple?
No. Apple replaces the full top case (Touch Bar, keyboard, battery, and trackpad). We repair just the Touch Bar. Our approach costs less than half of Apple's top case replacement and delivers the same result.

## Touch ID

### What We Do
We repair Touch ID on all Apple Silicon models (M1 to M4). The Touch ID button is both a power button and a biometric scanner, paired to the logic board. We use official Apple-sourced parts and submit the repair through Apple's authentication servers to retain your device's security features. We replace only the faulty component rather than the entire logic board.

### Key Facts
- Price: from £199 (includes Apple part registration and calibration)
- Turnaround: 2-3 working days standard; express available if pre-booked and part delivered
- Parts: original Apple Touch ID modules, registered through Apple's system
- Warranty: 2 years

### FAQs

#### Why is my Touch ID not working?
Could be internal damage from a drop, a crack in the sensor, or liquid damage near the power button. Touch ID is linked to the logic board; any break in the data line will stop it responding. We inspect both the button and the board.

#### Can Touch ID be replaced on a MacBook?
Yes, for Apple Silicon models. Apple now allows registered third-party repairers to calibrate these parts. For older Intel-based MacBooks, replacement is often not possible due to Apple's part pairing restrictions; we inspect the logic board for alternative repair options.

#### Can you repair Touch ID without replacing the logic board?
Yes, for Apple Silicon MacBooks. Even though Apple's own stores often quote for a logic board replacement, we replace only the Touch ID sensor. This saves significant time and cost.

#### Does the repair need to be pre-booked?
Yes. We confirm part compatibility with Apple using your MacBook's serial number before proceeding, so Touch ID repairs must be pre-booked.
