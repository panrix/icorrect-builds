## FAQs

### How do I know if my battery needs replacing?
Signs: fast drain, "Service Recommended" in macOS, cycle count over 1000, shutting off without warning, only works plugged in, swelling under trackpad.

### Do you use original Apple batteries?
Yes for newer models. For older models where originals are unavailable, we use highest-quality OEM batteries matched to Apple's voltage and charge profiles.

### Will I still see battery health in settings?
Yes. Our batteries support full battery health visibility: cycle count, maximum capacity, and condition.

### What is included in the replacement process?
Pre-repair diagnostic, safe discharge, battery install with correct adhesive and insulation, full charge and rundown cycle test, and post-repair quality check.

### Will a failing battery damage my MacBook?
It can. A degraded battery causes internal pressure (damaging trackpad, keyboard, screen) and power regulation issues that can affect the logic board.

### My MacBook only works when plugged in. Can that cause further damage?
Yes. The MacBook pulls current directly through the logic board, creating stress and heat that can cause board damage. Replace the battery to prevent this.
