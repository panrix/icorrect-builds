# iPhone Screen Repair

## What We Do
We replace iPhone screens using original Apple OLED displays reclaimed from genuine devices, tested and calibrated in-house. Screens are sourced from insurance write-offs with perfect displays. All screens go through pre-repair checks, post-repair checks, and final sign-off.

**We only fit genuine OLED screens. We do not offer aftermarket screens.**

## FAQs

### What screen do you fit?
Original Apple OLED, reclaimed from genuine devices. Same screen the iPhone shipped with. Full brightness, colour, and True Tone. We do not fit aftermarket, LCD, or hard OLED screens.

### Will I lose Face ID, True Tone, or Apple Pay?
No. All features work after repair. Genuine screens are calibrated through Apple's system.

### How do you source your screens?
From damaged iPhones declared unrepairable (logic board faults, etc.) sourced from insurance providers. The screens are perfect; we strip, test, clean, and recalibrate them.

### What does the warranty cover?
Covers screen faults: failed touch, ghost touches, flickering, blackouts. Does not cover accidental damage. If the part fails without damage, we replace it.

### My screen was replaced elsewhere and the colours look wrong.
Likely an LCD or hard OLED screen. We replace it with a genuine OLED to restore proper display quality.
