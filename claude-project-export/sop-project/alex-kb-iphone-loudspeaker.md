## FAQs

### How do I know if my loudspeaker needs replacing?
No sound during speakerphone calls, music, or video. Rattling, distortion, or crackling from the bottom speaker. Alarms or notifications too quiet or silent.

### What if there is no sound at all?
If both the loudspeaker and earpiece have no audio, we upgrade to a full diagnostic to check the audio IC and related power circuits.

### What causes loudspeakers to fail?
Age, moisture exposure, debris in the mesh, or internal damage from drops. Sometimes the audio signal path on the logic board is affected.

### Can a damaged speaker reduce resale value?
Yes. A phone with no speaker output is often marked as BER on trade-in platforms or graded lower.

### What does the warranty cover?
Two-year warranty covering the part and workmanship. Does not cover accidental damage or liquid ingress after repair.
