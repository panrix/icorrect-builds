# Advanced Diagnostics

## What We Do
We carry out component-level and microchip-level diagnostics to find the exact cause of device failure. Unlike most repair shops, we can test and repair at logic board level. Our process includes external power testing, DC power supply injection, thermal and visual inspection under microscope, and logic board circuit mapping using custom repair maps built from thousands of real-world repairs. You receive a clear report with the fault description, test data, photos, and a confirmed repair quote.

## Diagnostic Process
1. External power testing: measure current draw from charger to check power path behaviour
2. DC power supply injection: apply controlled power to battery terminals to simulate boot behaviour
3. Thermal and visual inspection: check for short circuits, corrosion, failed components under microscope
4. Logic board circuit mapping: test specific board areas based on symptoms using custom repair maps
5. Quote and report: clear breakdown with fault description, test data, and photos

## FAQs

### What types of issues require a diagnostic first?
Power failures, battery not charging, no display, random shutdowns, overheating, no sound, camera failures, or post-liquid damage. Essentially any fault where the cause is unclear or could have multiple origins.

### What do I get after the diagnostic?
A clear breakdown of the fault, supporting test data, photos if applicable, and a confirmed quote to complete the repair. If you choose not to proceed, your device is returned reassembled or you can recycle it with us.

### Will I be charged if you cannot repair the device?
Yes, the diagnostic fee still applies as it covers the full testing process. If the device is unrepairable, we can offer a trade-in value or data recovery options where possible.

### Is diagnostic testing safe for my data?
Yes. Our process is non-destructive and we prioritise data integrity. We do not wipe your device and take every precaution to protect your information.
