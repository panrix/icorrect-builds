# Apple Watch OLED Screen Replacement

## What We Do
We replace the entire display module (OLED panel, touch sensor, and frame) when the screen is black, shows lines, or has colour distortion. We use only original Apple Watch screens pulled from donor devices; no aftermarket screens. After installation, the adhesive is cured using factory-standard procedures and the watch is resealed to restore liquid resistance.

## FAQs

### How do I know if I need a full screen replacement?
If your display is completely black, shows lines, has colour distortion, or has dark patches under the glass, the OLED layer is damaged. If the touch response is gone but the watch still connects to your phone, it likely needs a full replacement.

### Will my Apple Watch still be water resistant?
Yes. We use the same strength adhesive Apple uses during manufacturing and reseal the watch for daily wear, showering, and swimming. We recommend avoiding extreme exposure like deep diving.

### Why is this more expensive than glass-only repair?
This is a full module replacement, not a refurbishment. The cost includes the original OLED screen, bonding materials, curing, and testing. It is still significantly more affordable than Apple's out-of-warranty replacement.

### Is it worth repairing or should I replace the watch?
In most cases, repairing is more cost-effective. Apple charges close to the cost of a new device for an out-of-warranty replacement. If your watch is functioning except for the screen, repair restores the value at a fraction of the price.
