## FAQs

### What are the signs my rear camera needs replacing?
Blurry photos, camera won't focus, black screen, black spots or fuzz in images, flickering or shaking. These indicate a faulty camera module.

### Can a cracked lens damage the rear camera?
Yes. A cracked lens exposes the module to dust and moisture. If there is blur or haze, the module may also need replacing. We check both lens and camera during intake.

### I had a rear glass repair and now the camera is foggy. Can this be fixed?
Common issue with poor rear glass replacements. If the seal was not restored, dust or moisture enters the camera. We inspect and replace as needed with original parts.

### Will the camera show as original after repair?
Yes. Calibrated through Apple's diagnostic tools, the repair is logged and the camera is confirmed as original. No warning messages in settings.

### What if the issue is deeper than the camera?
We upgrade to a diagnostic and check for logic board or circuit faults. Quote provided before proceeding.
