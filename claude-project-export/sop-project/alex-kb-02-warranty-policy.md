# KB-02: Warranty Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-03-04
**Status:** Confirmed — Ferrari approved 2026-03-11
**Priority:** HIGH (appears in 31 conversations)

---

## Overview

iCorrect offers a **2-year warranty on all repairs**, from the date of completion.

---

## What the Warranty Covers

- The specific repair carried out by iCorrect
- Parts installed by iCorrect during the repair
- Workmanship and installation quality

---

## What Voids the Warranty

The warranty is void if **any** of the following apply after the repair:

1. **New physical damage** — the device has been dropped, had liquid damage, or sustained any new physical damage since the repair
2. **Third-party repair** — the device has been opened or repaired by anyone other than iCorrect since our repair

---

## Claim Process

**All warranty claims route to human review. No commitment may be made to the client without a human check.**

When a customer raises a warranty claim:
1. Confirm: is the claim within 2 years of the repair completion date?
2. Confirm: does the fault match the original repair (same component/area)?
3. Check: any signs of new damage or third-party interference?
4. → Route to Human Escalation Queue with findings

---

## Agent Script

**Customer says their repair has failed / same fault has returned:**

> "Thank you for letting us know. All iCorrect repairs come with a 2-year warranty, so if the fault is related to the original repair, we'll take care of it. Let me check your repair record and get this reviewed — can I confirm the email address you booked under?"

Then: pull Monday.com record, check completion date, flag to human with full context.

**Do not** confirm or deny warranty eligibility to the customer before human review.
