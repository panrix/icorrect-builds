# MacBook Dustgate Repair

## What We Do
Dustgate is caused by dust particles entering the gap between the screen and housing, damaging the image signal flex cables over time. Symptoms: screen only works at certain angles, flickering, colour distortion, vertical lines. We repair or replace the affected flex cable under a microscope, preserving the original Apple display. No full screen replacement needed.

## FAQs

### Why does my screen only work at certain angles?
Key symptom of Dustgate. Dust damages signal lines in the flex cable inside the hinge. Opening or closing the lid shifts pressure, making the connection intermittent.

### What is the difference between Dustgate and Flexgate?
Different faults. Flexgate affects the backlight cable (screen goes black or shows stage light glow), found in 2016-2018 models. Dustgate affects image signal cables (flickering, colour distortion), typically in models made after 2018.

### Can Dustgate be repaired without replacing the screen?
Yes. We isolate and repair the faulty cable at component level. Most shops say a new screen is needed because they cannot do cable-level repair.

### Will Dustgate get worse over time?
Yes. Each lid open/close further damages the cable. Eventually leads to complete display failure.

### Does Apple cover Dustgate?
No. Unlike Flexgate, Apple has never officially acknowledged Dustgate. We recommend checking with Apple first; if they decline, we repair it.

### Is the repair covered by warranty?
Yes. Two-year warranty. We inspect and clean the hinge area to reduce risk of recurrence.
