# iPhone Rear Camera Lens Repair

## What We Do
We replace cracked or missing iPhone camera lenses using genuine sapphire glass with factory-spec UV bonding (not double-sided tape). A cracked lens allows dust and moisture into the camera housing, which can damage the camera module. Most repairs take under 2 hours. We inspect both lens and camera during intake; if the camera is also damaged, we quote separately.

## FAQs

### Can a cracked lens damage the rear camera?
Yes. Dust and moisture enter through cracks and can blur photos, damage internal coatings, or cause complete camera failure. Repair early to avoid a more expensive camera replacement.

### Do you use original parts?
Yes. Original Apple or reclaimed sapphire lenses, bonded with UV-cure adhesive matching Apple's manufacturing process. No cheap glass or stick-on lenses.

### Will a damaged lens affect trade-in value?
Yes. A cracked lens is considered physical damage and can result in BER grading. A £39-£59 repair can recover hundreds in trade-in value.

### I tried replacing it myself and it fell out. Can you fix it?
Yes. We remove leftover adhesive, clean the housing, and fit a genuine lens with proper alignment and UV curing.

### Does the lens replacement include camera calibration?
Not usually needed for lens-only repair. We still test focus, colour accuracy, and image quality before and after.
