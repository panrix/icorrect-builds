## FAQs

### What is a MacBook diagnostic?
A complete inspection and test to identify exactly what is wrong. We test power, charging, display, logic board, and more down to component level.

### My MacBook is dead. Can you still diagnose it?
Yes. We carry out board-level testing to find why. Often a failed power circuit, burnt chip, or liquid damage.

### Apple said it is unrepairable. Can you still check?
Yes. Apple only runs modular checks. If the board has failed, they replace it. We go to microchip level, which is why we often recover devices others write off.

### How long does it take?
2-3 working days. Express diagnostic available on request.

### Will I get a full report?
Yes. Breakdown of findings, cause of issue, and confirmed repair quote. Liquid damage cases include photos.

### What if my MacBook cannot be repaired?
We offer a trade-in value against a replacement. We preserve data whenever possible.

### Do I lose anything if I don't proceed?
No. You keep the report. We can offer recycling, trade-in, or upgrade options.
