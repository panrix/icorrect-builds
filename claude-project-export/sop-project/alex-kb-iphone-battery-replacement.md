Every replacement includes pre-fit rundown tests to confirm the battery actually needs replacing, and post-repair testing for charging, reporting, and thermal output. Most replacements are completed in under two hours. For older models where originals are unavailable, we use highest-grade OEM batteries.

## FAQs

### How do I know if my battery needs replacing?
Replace when battery health drops below 85%. Signs: sluggish performance, random shutdowns, overheating, poor charge retention. We run a full diagnostic to confirm before proceeding.

### Will my iPhone still show battery health after replacement?
Yes. Because we use genuine batteries with original BMS, all data reads properly: battery health, cycle count, serial number, and installation date.

### Do you use genuine Apple batteries?
Yes for most models. For older models where originals are unavailable, we use highest-quality OEM batteries with original BMS. We never use cheap cells or spoof readings.

### Can you replace a swollen battery safely?
Yes. Swollen batteries are removed carefully using heat, isopropanol, and non-metal tools following Apple's fire safety guidelines. Do not attempt this yourself.

### Is it safe to replace if the phone was repaired before?
Yes. We remove any previous aftermarket battery, fit a genuine one with original BMS, and calibrate through Apple's system. Full battery tracking is restored.

### What does the warranty cover?
Covers faults with the battery or workmanship: early shutdowns, reading errors, poor charge retention. Does not cover accidental damage or normal wear over time.

### Can a bad battery cause other issues?
Yes. A degraded battery causes throttled performance, freezing, random shutdowns, and overheating. Replacing it can make the iPhone feel new again.

### My battery was replaced elsewhere but still drains fast. Can you help?
Yes. We run a full diagnostic to find the real issue. Often the wrong battery was installed, or post-repair testing was skipped, or the fault is not the battery at all.
