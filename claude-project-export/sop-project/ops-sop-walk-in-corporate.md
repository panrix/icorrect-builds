# SOP: Walk-In Corporate Repair

**Status:** Draft v1 (compiled from Ricky Q&A, 2026-04-06)
**Scope:** Walk-in corporate clients. Follows the same flow as walk-in simple repair with the following differences.

**Base flow:** See [Walk-In Simple Repair](sop-walk-in-simple-repair.md)

---

## Differences from Standard Walk-In

### Payment
- **No money discussion with the client.** Corporate repairs are invoiced to the company, not paid upfront.

### Priority
- **Corporate repairs are always prioritised.** Top of the queue ahead of standard walk-ins.

### Intake Pre-Checks (must confirm before accepting device)
1. Is it a simple repair or diagnostic?
2. If diagnostic: are they aware of the turnaround time?
3. Do we have a passcode agreement with this company? If not, do we have the passcode?
4. Has any testing been completed by their IT department already?
5. Will the same individual be collecting, or someone else?

### Delivery Option
- Some corporates have the option for courier delivery after repair (device shipped back instead of collected)
- Must ask at intake whether they want collection or delivery
- **Note from Ricky:** courier delivery should be offered to ALL clients, not just corporates. To be added to the standard questionnaire.

### Passcode
- Some corporate accounts have an agreement that we don't take passcodes (we accept the testing risk)
- Must check before intake whether this agreement exists for this company

---

## Rest of Flow

Labelling, queue assignment, repair, QC, and collection/delivery all follow the standard walk-in simple repair SOP, except with priority queue position.
