## FAQs

### How do I know if my iPad battery needs replacing?
If your iPad drains quickly, shuts off randomly, feels sluggish, or the screen is lifting (sign of swelling), the battery is likely degraded. We run a full diagnostic to confirm before proceeding.

### What testing do you do on iPad batteries?
Pre-repair: battery health, cycle count, and voltage behaviour. Post-repair: full rundown test, charge cycle, and thermal check. This ensures the battery performs to spec before we return the iPad.

### Can a bad battery damage the screen or logic board?
Yes. A swelling battery can press against the screen, causing lifting, cracking, or backlight damage. In rare cases, voltage spikes from a failing battery can damage logic board components. Best to replace it early.

### Will my iPad performance improve after replacement?
Yes. A healthy battery helps maintain processor speed, app responsiveness, and overall stability. Many clients notice their iPads feel noticeably faster after replacement.
