## FAQs

### Can you repair the front camera without losing Face ID?
Yes on iPhone 14 and newer. Apple allows module replacement with calibration to retain all features. On older models, if Face ID sensors are already damaged, it cannot be restored.

### Why has my front camera stopped working?
Common causes: dust or moisture ingress, impact damage, or faulty screen replacement affecting the camera module.

### Can a bad screen replacement affect the front camera?
Yes. Poor sealing can let moisture or dust reach the camera lens. Fog, focus issues, or white haze in selfies are common symptoms.

### Will the camera show as original after repair?
Yes. Apple's calibration tools pair the new module and confirm it as original in settings.

### Can you fix failed front camera repairs from other shops?
Yes. We assess internal damage, replace what is needed, and recalibrate correctly.
