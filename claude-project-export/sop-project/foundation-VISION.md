# VISION.md — The Vision

## What We're Building

iCorrect will be known as the company that completes repairs to a level nobody else in the industry can match. Not just good repairs — the standard. The brand that other companies, insurers, networks, and the biggest names in tech look at and say: that's the company.

## The Mission

Save devices from landfill by bringing microelectronic repair from an advanced, specialist skill to a standardised, systemised process. Raise the entire industry's standard by proving that board-level diagnostics and repair can be done consistently, reliably, and at scale — not just by one gifted technician, but through systems that guide any trained engineer to that level.

## The Position

Specialist MacBook repair. That's the identity. iCorrect is the absolute authority on MacBook repair — pioneering the methods, leading the research, and pushing repair techniques out to the wider industry. We don't just fix what Apple says can't be fixed. We set the standard for how it should be done.

## The Edge

Ricky knows this industry and its systems better than anyone playing at this level. He's spoken to the insurers and the networks — nobody has the knowledge he has. They're playing bigger numbers, but they're not playing the same game. iCorrect is a team of 7 competing with companies ten times its size, and winning through technology, systems, and depth of expertise.

## The Growth Model

Sustainable. Every hire proven before it's made. Every system built before it's needed. Growth that compounds rather than oscillates. No more cycles of growth and decline — consistent forward movement driven by better processes, better tools, and agents that execute at the pace Ricky thinks.

## The Longer Term

When iCorrect is profitable, systemised, and self-sustaining, the model expands. Micro private equity — acquire other repair brands, apply the Mission Control playbook, raise their standard, grow them. Build a portfolio of repair businesses that all operate at the level iCorrect has set.

But that's after. The next 12 months are locked in on iCorrect.

## The Non-Negotiable

Ricky will not become his father — working all the time at the expense of family. He moved to Bali to be a family man first. The vision only works if it's built in a way where Ricky spends his ideas and his AI agents do the execution. Present for his family. Present for his business. Not consumed by either at the expense of the other.

This is what Mission Control is for. Not just operational efficiency — it's the architecture that makes the whole vision possible without sacrificing what matters most.
