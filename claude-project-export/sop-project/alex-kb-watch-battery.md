## FAQs

### How do I know if my Apple Watch battery needs replacing?
If your watch runs out of charge quickly, shuts off unexpectedly, becomes slow to respond, or does not last a full day. A swelling battery can lift the screen or cause the case to separate. We run a full diagnostic to confirm.

### Will my Apple Watch still be water resistant after replacement?
Yes. We reseal every watch using adhesives that match Apple's factory bonding process. The device is safe for showers and general water exposure.

### Will performance improve with a new battery?
Yes. A fresh battery restores energy flow, responsiveness, and Bluetooth/Wi-Fi stability. Many clients notice faster load times and more consistent connection to their iPhone.

### Can a bad battery damage the screen?
Yes. A swollen battery puts pressure on the screen, causing lifting, cracking, or permanent separation. We recommend replacing a faulty battery early to prevent further damage.
