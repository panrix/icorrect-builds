## FAQs

### My iPad is completely dead. Can you still test it?
Yes. We can trace the power pathway and locate the cause even with no signs of life. This could be a failed battery, charging port, or damage to the power management circuit.

### Can you diagnose faults Apple said were unrepairable?
Yes. Apple's diagnostics are surface-level. If the issue is on the logic board, they often recommend full device replacement. We test individual components at micro level, which is how we often repair what Apple writes off.

### What if the logic board is damaged?
We provide a clear quote for board-level repair. We do not automatically suggest replacements. We have one of the highest board repair success rates in the industry.

### What if I decide not to proceed with the repair?
The £49 diagnostic fee still applies. We also offer a trade-in option where you can recycle the iPad with us and receive a value based on its condition.
