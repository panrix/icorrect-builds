# Apple Watch Glass-Only Repair

## What We Do
We replace just the cracked outer glass while keeping the original OLED display underneath. We separate the damaged layer from the OLED panel, then bond and cure a replacement glass using original pulled glass and the same UV bonding process Apple uses in their factories. This retains liquid resistance for everyday use including showers and swimming. iCorrect pioneered third-party Apple Watch resealing and developed a reliable method for restoring liquid resistance, tested using pressure chamber testing.

## FAQs

### How do I know if I need glass-only or full screen repair?
If your display still shows image and responds to touch but the outer glass is cracked, you are a candidate for glass-only repair. If the screen is black, flickering, or unresponsive, a full OLED replacement may be required. We test and confirm before proceeding.

### Will my Apple Watch still be water resistant?
Yes, for standard use. Our sealing method matches Apple's original process and retains water resistance for showers, rain, and swimming. We do not recommend deep water dives or extended saltwater use.

### Why is your service more expensive than some others?
We use original glass, bond it using factory-standard methods, and reseal for liquid resistance. Cheaper services often use generic glass and adhesive, which are more likely to fall out, fog up, or break again.

### Can a broken glass damage the display underneath?
Yes. If left too long, cracks can allow dust or moisture to reach the OLED, damaging the internal panel or causing touch sensitivity issues. We recommend repairing early.
