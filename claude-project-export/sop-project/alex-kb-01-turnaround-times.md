## Overview

iCorrect offers tiered turnaround options. The available tiers depend on the device and repair type.

---

---

## Key Rules

- **Fast and Fastest tiers apply to Apple Silicon MacBook Screen Repairs only.** Intel models: Standard only.
- Always confirm MacBook chip generation (Apple Silicon vs Intel) before quoting a turnaround tier.
- Use the A-number (e.g. A2338) as the definitive identifier for MacBook model — critical for tier eligibility.
- Turnaround starts from the point the device is received by iCorrect.

---

## How to Identify Apple Silicon vs Intel

- Apple Silicon: M1, M2, M3, M4 chips — all MacBooks from late 2020 onwards
- Intel: Any MacBook made before late 2020
- If unsure: ask the customer for the A-number (found on the bottom of the device) or the year of purchase
