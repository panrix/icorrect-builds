## FAQs

### What is an iPhone diagnostic?
A complete test to find the exact fault. We check battery, charging port, display, logic board, and internal circuits. Essential when the iPhone is dead, has power issues, or has unknown faults.

### My iPhone is completely dead. Can you still diagnose it?
Yes. Dead iPhones are one of the most common cases we handle. Whether it is a failed charging IC, liquid damage, or logic board fault, we go deep enough to find the cause.

### Apple said my iPhone is unrepairable. Can you still check?
Yes. Apple does not do board-level diagnostics. If the logic board is at fault, they quote for whole-device replacement. We isolate and repair the actual failed component.

### Will I get a report?
Yes. Summary of findings, cause of failure, and confirmed repair quote. Liquid damage cases include photos.

### What if my iPhone cannot be repaired?
We offer a trade-in value or replacement device depending on stock. In most cases, we can repair what others cannot.

### Do I lose anything if I decide not to proceed?
No. You keep the report. We can offer recycling or upgrade options. Device can be returned untouched after testing.
