# iPhone Rear Glass Repair

## What We Do
Two repair types depending on model. **Modular models** (iPhone 14, 14 Plus, 15, 16 series): rear glass is removable and replaced as a standalone piece. **Fused models** (iPhone 14 Pro and earlier): rear glass is bonded to the housing, so the full rear housing is replaced. We do not offer laser glass removal on fused models as it risks internal damage. All repairs use original Apple parts and restore structural integrity and water resistance.

## FAQs

### Can you replace just the rear glass?
Only on supported models (iPhone 14, 14 Plus, 15, 16 series). On older models, the glass is fused to the frame so the full housing must be replaced.

### Can cracked rear glass cause other problems?
Yes. Cracks let moisture and dust in, which can affect the camera, wireless charging, or internal shielding. Best to repair before it leads to bigger issues.

### Can rear glass damage affect wireless charging or camera?
Yes. The rear panel protects and aligns the wireless charging coil and camera assembly. Cracks can cause charging failures, overheating, or camera fog.

### Will my iPhone still be water resistant after repair?
Yes. We reseal using the same strength adhesive Apple uses. However, we advise against full submersion after any third-party repair.

### Do you use original parts?
Yes. All rear glass and housing replacements are original parts: reclaimed from donors or sourced from authorised channels. No aftermarket glass or incomplete shells.

### Can you fix a failed rear glass repair from another shop?
Yes. We often see laser removal repairs with internal dust, fogged cameras, or short circuits. We clean the internals and restore with the correct part.
