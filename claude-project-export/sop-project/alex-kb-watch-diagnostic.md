# Apple Watch Diagnostic

## What We Do
We carry out full diagnostics on all Apple Watch models, checking power intake, battery health, and internal components. We identify faults including charging failures, sensor issues, and battery swelling. Logic board repairs are not usually feasible on Apple Watch due to Apple's hardened coating on the board. In those cases, we advise if replacement makes more financial sense.

## FAQs

### My Apple Watch is not charging. Can you help?
Yes. Charging issues are one of the most common faults. We test both the charging circuit and the battery to confirm where the issue lies and provide a quote if fixable.

### Can you fix logic board faults on Apple Watch?
Unfortunately not. Apple Watch logic boards are sealed with a hardened coating, making them extremely difficult to access or repair. If the issue is traced to the logic board, we will advise clearly and recommend replacement if more economical.

### What kind of faults can you fix?
Battery failure, charging circuit faults, unresponsive screens, and sensor-related issues such as heart rate monitor failure. We use original parts and proper testing for all repairs.

### What if I decide not to proceed?
No further cost beyond the £29 diagnostic fee. We also offer trade-in value for your damaged Apple Watch if you decide to upgrade.
