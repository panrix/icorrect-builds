# MacBook Screen Repair

## What We Do
We replace broken MacBook screens using genuine Apple displays reclaimed from donor devices. Instead of replacing the full lid assembly like Apple does, we replace only the damaged display panel, keeping the original housing, webcam, hinge, and lid intact. Every screen is recalibrated to factory colour, brightness, and tone standards. This approach costs 30-50% less than Apple and preserves features like True Tone and full brightness.

## FAQs

### Will I keep True Tone and other features after the repair?
Yes. We only use genuine Apple displays, so True Tone, full brightness, native resolution, colour calibration, and ambient light features are all retained. Every screen is tested and calibrated during final QC.

### Do you use Apple screens?
Yes. Original Apple displays reclaimed from genuine MacBooks. Every screen is tested and recalibrated to Apple factory specs. We never use aftermarket panels.

### Will it be obvious my screen was repaired?
No. The display will look exactly as it did before the damage. We use Apple screens and precision tools to ensure the fit and finish are flawless.

### Can you repair Liquid Retina XDR or high-spec screens?
Yes. We handle all screen types including Liquid Retina XDR on 14-inch and 16-inch MacBook Pros. These displays require greater precision due to their mini-LED structure and calibration demands.

### What if the screen isn't the only issue?
We always check for deeper faults before beginning any screen repair. If your MacBook does not power on or show a display, we upgrade to a full diagnostic first. You will get a clear quote before we proceed.

### What if I had my screen repaired elsewhere and it's still faulty?
We deal with these cases often. Poor third-party repairs frequently use non-original screens with dim displays, missing features, and poor fit. We can replace the faulty screen with an original one and restore your MacBook to full health.
