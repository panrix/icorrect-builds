# SPEC Template

## 1) Problem Statement
- Business problem
- Current state pain
- Why now

## 2) Business Case and Success Metrics
- Expected impact
- KPIs and targets
- Measurement cadence

## 3) Scope
### In Scope
- Itemized deliverables

### Out of Scope
- Explicit deferrals

## 4) Architecture
- High-level components
- Data flow
- Deployment shape

## 5) Data Model and Integrations
- Core entities/tables
- External systems touched
- Read/write boundaries

## 6) Hard Rules and Constraints
- Non-negotiables
- Security/compliance constraints
- Operational constraints

## 7) Phase Plan and Acceptance Criteria
- Phase breakdown
- Deliverables per phase
- Done criteria per phase

## 8) Risks and Blockers
- Known risks
- Mitigations
- Current blockers

## 9) Open Questions
- Unresolved questions
- Owner
- Decision deadline

## 10) Source Map / Provenance
- Source document list
- Section-to-source mapping
